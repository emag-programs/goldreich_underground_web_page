About Us

Mr Eden M. Almog-Goldreich P.Eng CEng MICE, is an engineer with over 20 years global experience equally shared between consulting and contracting in the field of underground works. Mr Almog-Goldreich unique skills include advanced numerical analysis and development of innovative calculation and design methodologies implemented on highly complex jobs worldwide.

Through Goldreich Underground Engineering Inc., Mr Almog-Goldreich provides the following specialized services for consultants, contractors and owners in any location around the world:

  • Subject Matter Expert (SME) services including but not limited to Independent Checker, Constructability Reviews and Expert Witness.
  • Development of new analytical tools and methodologies bespoke to a particular project which can be used for design verification purposes
  • Review of technical requirements and baseline reports at project setup or construction phases.
  • Mentorship and training for young engineers or those transitioning from other specialties into the discipline of tunnelling and underground construction.
